All Together

First, radically accept human nature.

Then, learn to create value for society by combining foundational knowledge (learned through exposure to markets and physics) with specialised, cutting-edge knowledge.

Apply this knowledge creatively to solve problems that nobody else in the world could've tackled.

Use these unique solutions to help other people (and hence build a solid reputation).

Respectfully question the constraints that most assume to be true. Then abstract out the durable compounding mechanisms at play, and plow as many resources as you can towards them.

Don't be discouraged by people around you who get “lucky breaks”.

Extend your time horizon as far as you can into the future.

And if you do all this, you’ll never have to worry about being replaced (for as long as replacement is a worthwhile worry).

There’s only one question left: what’s next?
