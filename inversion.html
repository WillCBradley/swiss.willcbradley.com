Inversion

Nobody is infallible.

The principles we've just covered are reliable (as far as I can tell), but they're vulnerable to human error.

Here's an ongoing list of some such errors (so you can invert them). I'll add to this as I run into more of them:

  • Overextend your resources until you can't risk (or don't have time for) exposure to truth
  • Base your reputation on transient phenomena
  • Be impatient
  • Build your compounding mechanisms some time in the future (rather than today)
  • Refuse to see the world for what it is
